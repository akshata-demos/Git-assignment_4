aloo tikkiAloo tikki, a type of chaat.
Chaat is an umbrella term for a wide range of roadside foods that usually feature some kind
of fried dough with various ingredients that typically create a spicy, tangy, or salty
flavour, though some chaat are sweet. Papri chaat (or papdi chaat) is crispy fried-dough
wafers served with typical chaat ingredients such as chickpeas, boiled potatoes, yogurt sauce,
and tamarind and coriander chutneys; it may also contain pomegranate seeds and sev (noodles
made from fried gram flour). Aloo tikki is a golden fried-potato patty that is often stuffed
with peas or dal and served with a variety of spicy chutneys and sometimes chickpeas, while
aloo chaat is simply boiled potatoes that are cubed, fried, seasoned, and served hot.
